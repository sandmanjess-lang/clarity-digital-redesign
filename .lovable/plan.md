

## Clarity Group — Modern Redesign

A professional, modern multi-page website for Clarity Group (corporate finance advisory) using their brand colors: **slate blue-grey (#43535f)** and **green accent (#7aa428)**, with a glass & gradient aesthetic.

### Design System
- **Primary**: Slate blue-grey (#43535f) for dark sections/backgrounds
- **Accent**: Green (#7aa428) for CTAs, highlights, and active states
- **Style**: Glassmorphism panels (backdrop-blur, semi-transparent cards), subtle gradients, smooth hover animations
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large headings with light weight

### Pages

#### 1. Home Page
- Full-width hero section with gradient overlay (dark slate → transparent), bold headline "Investment & Corporate Finance Solutions", green CTA button
- Brief intro text about Clarity Advisors
- 3 glass-effect service cards (M&A, Capital Raising, Valuations)
- "Why Clarity" section highlighting key differentiators (experience, direct access, personalised approach)

#### 2. About Page
- Content from original site about the firm's focus on advising unlisted corporates
- Key stats or highlights in glass cards (e.g., "20+ years experience", "Global network")
- Services breakdown: capital raising, M&A, disposals, BEE, valuations, strategic consulting

#### 3. People Page
- Team member cards with glass-effect backgrounds
- **Don Millar** — bio with role, background, expertise
- **Valecia van Rensburg** — bio with qualifications and experience
- Professional placeholder avatars

#### 4. Transactions Page
- List of notable transactions as a timeline or card grid
- Each transaction as a glass card with green accent bullet
- All 11 transactions from the original site

#### 5. Contact Page
- Contact details: office address, phone, email
- Clean layout with a glass card for contact info
- Embedded or linked Google Map for Illovo, Johannesburg location

### Shared Components
- **Navigation bar**: Sticky header with logo text "clarity group", nav links (Home, About, People, Transactions, Contact), green active indicator
- **Footer**: Minimal footer with copyright and links
- Smooth page transitions and hover effects throughout
